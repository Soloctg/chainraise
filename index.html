About Irogon
GoFundMe Digital Asset Exchange

Irogon is a decentralized fundraising platform built on Solana. We help creators, communities, and nonprofits raise funds with trustless smart contracts, ensuring transparency, accountability, and global reach.


Irogon is a premier capital raising platform that connects qualified investors worldwide with progressive companies. We are committed to revolutionizing how startups are funded by providing an innovative solution for those wanting to invest in Web3.0, real estate, and other startup businesses. Our goal is to connect any type of investor to a diverse range of opportunities, ensuring compliance and transparency through on-chain processes.
